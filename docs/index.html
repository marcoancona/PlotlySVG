Plotly-SVG

A Chrome-specific bookmarklet that extracts and downloads SVG plots generated by Plotly offline

Add me to bookmarks

How it works

Drag the button above into your bookmark bar in Chrome.
Open a Plotly plot and click on the bookmark to download the plot in SVG format.
Done.
